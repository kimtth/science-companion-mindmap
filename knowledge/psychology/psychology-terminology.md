# Psychology Terminology

## Core Concepts

### Biological Bases of Behavior
- **Neuron**: Nerve cell; dendrites receive, axon transmits; synapse between neurons
- **Neurotransmitter**: Chemical messenger across synaptic gap; serotonin, dopamine, acetylcholine
- **Central Nervous System (CNS)**: Brain and spinal cord; processes information
- **Peripheral Nervous System (PNS)**: Nerves outside CNS; somatic (voluntary) and autonomic (involuntary)
- **Brain Structures**: Cerebral cortex (higher functions), hippocampus (memory), amygdala (emotion), cerebellum (coordination)
- **Hemispheric Specialization**: Left (language, logic), right (spatial, creativity); connected by corpus callosum

### Cognitive Psychology
- **Memory**: Encoding, storage, retrieval; sensory → short-term → long-term
- **Working Memory**: Active manipulation of information; limited capacity (~7±2 items)
- **Long-Term Memory**: Declarative (explicit: episodic + semantic) and procedural (implicit)
- **Attention**: Selective focus; limited resource; cocktail party effect
- **Perception**: Organization and interpretation of sensory information
- **Problem Solving**: Algorithms (systematic) vs heuristics (shortcuts)
- **Cognitive Bias**: Systematic errors in thinking; confirmation bias, anchoring, availability heuristic

### Developmental Psychology
- **Piaget's Stages**: Sensorimotor (0-2), preoperational (2-7), concrete operational (7-11), formal operational (11+)
- **Erikson's Stages**: 8 psychosocial stages from trust vs mistrust to integrity vs despair
- **Attachment Theory (Bowlby)**: Secure, anxious-ambivalent, avoidant, disorganized attachment styles
- **Nature vs Nurture**: Genetics vs environment; interactionist view predominates

### Social Psychology
- **Conformity**: Adjusting behavior to match group norms; Asch experiment
- **Obedience**: Following orders from authority figure; Milgram experiment
- **Attribution Theory**: Explaining causes of behavior; internal (dispositional) vs external (situational)
- **Fundamental Attribution Error**: Overemphasizing personality, underemphasizing situation for others' behavior
- **Groupthink**: Group prioritizes consensus over critical thinking
- **Bystander Effect**: Less likely to help when others are present; diffusion of responsibility

### Abnormal Psychology
- **DSM-5**: Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders; classification system
- **Anxiety Disorders**: Generalized anxiety, panic, phobias, social anxiety, OCD
- **Mood Disorders**: Major depressive disorder, bipolar disorder
- **Schizophrenia**: Psychotic disorder; hallucinations, delusions, disorganized thinking
- **Personality Disorders**: Enduring patterns of behavior; borderline, antisocial, narcissistic
- **Therapies**: Cognitive-behavioral (CBT), psychodynamic, humanistic, medication (SSRIs, antipsychotics)

### Learning and Conditioning
- **Classical Conditioning (Pavlov)**: Neutral stimulus + unconditioned stimulus → conditioned response
- **Operant Conditioning (Skinner)**: Reinforcement (increases behavior) and punishment (decreases behavior)
- **Observational Learning (Bandura)**: Learning by watching others; modeling; Bobo doll experiment
- **Reinforcement Schedules**: Fixed/variable ratio, fixed/variable interval
